Eva Lendel

Eva Lendel is a young brand on the wedding fashion market. It was launched in 2015 in Los Angeles with the aim of creating and bringing something new and exclusive to the wedding fashion world.

The main idea of the brand is a creation of wedding dresses due to the latest tendencies of world wedding industry. Eva Lendels’ bride – it is a modern girl who follows the latest fashion trends, likes experiments, and can make an original and innovative decisions offered by the brand.

Using the latest fashion trends in combination with interesting and feminine styles together create an exclusive dress about which every bride dreams. Charm, mystery, sensuality, romance, all these epithets characterize the brand.
